Visit Jupiter

Pay a visit to Jupiter, Florida, with its picturesque coastal beauty, pristine beaches, and charming small-town atmosphere.

Top three activities to do in Jupiter

Get some Sun at the beach

Indulge in the ultimate relaxation as you lounge on Jupiter Beach's pristine shores, where the warm golden sands meet the gentle, azure waves of the Atlantic Ocean, providing the perfect sun-soaked escape with endless opportunities for sun-kissed bliss.

Rent a Kayak

Whether you're exploring the Intracoastal Waterway, paddling along the Loxahatchee River, or venturing into the lush, winding water trails of Jonathan Dickinson State Park, you'll encounter a rich tapestry of wildlife, including manatees, dolphins, colorful fish, and various bird species.

Explore the Lighthouse

Experience the thrill of ascending the historic Jupiter Inlet Lighthouse's 105 steps, where breathtaking panoramic views of the sparkling Atlantic Ocean and picturesque Intracoastal Waterway await, offering a memory-making adventure that combines history, culture, and awe-inspiring vistas in one unforgettable visit.
